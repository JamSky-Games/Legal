## 1. About this Privacy Policy

This Policy applies to JamSky's Services, including our games (such as Incorgnito), beta builds (when offered), support channels, contests, official Discord servers and other JamSky-operated communities, and our website at `jamskygames.com`.

## 2. Information we collect & how

We collect information in three primary ways: information you provide to us, information collected automatically when you use the Services, and information from third-party sources.

### 2.1 Information you provide

- **Account information:** display name, email address (for account recovery and support), and any social-platform identifier you use to sign in (such as Apple ID, Google account, or Facebook account).
- **Date of birth:** collected at first launch as part of our age-gate. We use this information to enforce our minimum-age requirement (see Section 10) and limit retention to what is needed for that purpose.
- **Purchase information:** items purchased, prices, and Storefront receipts. **Payment card information is processed directly by Apple App Store or Google Play and is not collected, stored, or accessible by JamSky.**
- **Support communications:** emails, support tickets, attachments, and any information you choose to provide when contacting us.
- **Survey, contest, and marketing-opt-in responses** when you choose to provide them.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically

- **Device & technical data:** device model, operating system version, language preference, screen resolution, country (derived from IP address — we do not retain full IP addresses with your Account beyond what is needed for security), advertising identifier (subject to your consent — see Section 6), carrier name, and timezone.
- **Gameplay data:** session start/stop times, level progression, achievements, virtual-item balances (Bones, Zoomies), purchase events, and game-mode usage. Specific event names use a structured analytics schema (`snake_case`).
- **Approximate location:** derived from IP address (country and region only). We do not collect precise GPS location.
- **Anti-cheat & security signals:** device fingerprints, anomalous-behavior flags, server-side validation events. These signals are retained for up to **12 months** and used solely to protect the integrity of the Services.
- **Crash and performance logs.**
- **Inferences:** information we derive from the categories above (for example, predictions about your gameplay preferences, content you may enjoy, or your propensity to make in-Game purchases). Inferences are treated as personal information under California (CCPA/CPRA) and similar U.S. state laws.

### 2.3 Information from cookies and similar technologies

We and approved partners use cookies, software development kits ("SDKs"), pixels, and similar technologies to:

- Remember your preferences and keep you logged in;
- Measure Service performance and detect crashes;
- Understand aggregate usage patterns;
- Deliver, personalize, and measure advertising (subject to your consent and applicable law — see Sections 5 and 6).

You can control these technologies through your device or browser settings. See Section 5 for details.

### 2.4 Information from third-party sources

When you connect a third-party service to your Account (for example, signing in with Apple, Google, or Facebook), we may receive information from that service in accordance with that service's policies and your settings — typically a unique identifier, your username, and (if you authorize) your email address.

We may also receive aggregated demographic or attribution information from advertising and analytics partners.

### 2.5 What we do **not** collect at this time

We are committed to data minimization. As of the date of this Policy:

- We do **not** offer in-game chat, forums, or other free-text communication between players.
- We do **not** collect uploaded avatar images, photographs, or other media uploaded by users.
- We do **not** collect biometric data, precise GPS location, contact lists, or microphone or camera input.
- We do **not** knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16 (see Section 10).

If we change any of the practices above, we will update this Policy and provide reasonable notice.

### 2.6 Sensitive personal information

Under California (CCPA/CPRA) and similar U.S. state privacy laws, certain personal information is classified as "**sensitive personal information**." JamSky collects only one category of sensitive personal information: **Account login credentials** (your password and any associated authentication tokens), which we collect and use solely to provide and secure the Service.

We do not use or disclose sensitive personal information for purposes other than those that are necessary to provide the Service or that are otherwise permitted under applicable state law (such as fraud prevention, security, or compliance with legal obligations). We do not "sell" or "share" sensitive personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, and you do not need to opt out separately for sensitive personal information. Sensitive personal information is retained only for the period needed for the purposes described above and in accordance with Section 8.

## 5. Advertising, analytics, and tracking technologies

We and our advertising partners may use cookies, advertising identifiers, SDKs, pixels, and similar technologies to deliver and measure advertising and to understand usage of the Services. This may include "interest-based" or "personalized" advertising, where ads shown to you are selected based on your inferred interests.

### Your choices

- **In-Game:** toggle "Personalized Ads" in **Settings → Privacy** within the Game.
- **Apple iOS:** disable App Tracking Transparency for JamSky in **iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking** (also see Section 6 below).

- **Direct request:** email [privacy@jamskygames.com](mailto:privacy@jamskygames.com) with subject "Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising."

We do not knowingly enable interest-based advertising for users under 16.

## 8. Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as we need it for the purposes described in this Policy, including providing the Services, complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, enforcing our Terms, and preventing fraud.

The retention periods we apply are summarized in the table below. Periods may be extended where required or permitted by law (for example, for ongoing investigations or legal claims).

| Data category | Retention | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Account data (display name, email, social-login ID) | Active period + 24 months after last activity | Account inactivity triggers deletion review |
| Date-of-birth used for age-gate (16+ users) | Year of birth retained at Account level; not exposed in Services | Used only for age enforcement |
| Under-16 age-gate-failure record (hashed device fingerprint + country code) | Up to 60 months (5 years) | Used solely to prevent re-registration and as evidence of compliance |
| Gameplay analytics (event-level) | Up to 25 months | Rolling deletion |
| Crash logs | Up to 90 days | Rolling deletion |
| Anti-cheat & security signals (device fingerprints, anomaly flags) | Up to 12 months | Used solely for fraud and integrity |
| Purchase / receipt records | 7 years | Tax and audit retention |
| Support tickets and correspondence | 24 months from closure | Longer if subject of complaint or claim |
| Marketing-opt-in records | Until withdrawal of consent + 24 months | Audit trail for consent management |
| Aggregated / de-identified information | Indefinite | No longer identifies you |

After applicable retention periods, we delete or de-identify the information.

## 9. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. These include:

- **Encryption in transit** using TLS 1.2+ for communications between the Game and our servers;
- **Encryption at rest** for sensitive data fields stored on our infrastructure;
- **Role-based access controls** limiting access to personal information to authorized personnel with a business need;
- **Vendor due diligence** for third-party processors and service providers;
- **Routine logging, monitoring, and review** of security-relevant events;
- **Storefront-managed payment processing** — we do not directly handle credit card numbers;
- **Server-side validation** of game state and anti-cheat signals.

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a personal-data breach:

- **Where required by applicable law**, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within the legally required timeframe (for example, within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, where GDPR Article 33 applies, once we offer the Services in those regions; within timeframes applicable to U.S. state laws, including California).
- **Where a breach is likely to result in a high risk to your rights and freedoms**, we will notify affected users without undue delay, as required by applicable law (including GDPR Article 34 where applicable).

To report a security concern, contact [security@jamskygames.com](mailto:security@jamskygames.com).

## 10. Children — under 16

**Our Services are not directed to anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16 anywhere in the world.**

We use a neutral age-gate at first launch that asks for your date of birth. If the age-gate indicates you are under 16:

- You will not be able to create an Account or access the Services;
- We **immediately discard** the full date of birth you provided;
- We retain only a hashed device fingerprint and your country code, used solely to prevent re-attempts at the age-gate and to localize the block messaging. The retention period for this minimal data is set out in Section 8.

If the age-gate indicates you are 16 or older, only the year of birth is retained at the Account level (per Section 8) for age-enforcement purposes; the full date is not retained.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us at [privacy@jamskygames.com](mailto:privacy@jamskygames.com) so we can promptly delete the information and close the Account. We treat reports of minor-user accounts as a priority. **Once we confirm an Account belongs to a user under 16, we suspend the Account immediately upon receipt of a credible report and delete the personal information associated with that Account within seven (7) days of confirmation.**

We do not knowingly enable interest-based advertising or targeted-marketing communications for users we know or have reason to believe are under 16.

**No parental controls.** Because the Service requires all users to be at least 16 years old, JamSky does not offer parental controls or family-account features. Parents who wish to control or restrict a minor's use of mobile devices generally should use device-level parental controls (Apple Screen Time, Google Family Link, or similar) and/or storefront-level family controls (Apple Family Sharing, Google Play Family Library).
