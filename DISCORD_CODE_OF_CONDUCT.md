# JamSky Games Discord — Code of Conduct

_Last updated: 2026-04-27_

> Post this in your Discord server's `#rules` channel. Configure Discord AutoMod (free tier) to enforce keyword filters and spam blocks alongside these rules.

---

## Welcome

This is the official JamSky Games Discord server. We're glad you're here. To keep this a fun place to talk about Incorgnito, share strategies, and meet other players, all members agree to follow this Code of Conduct. By staying in this server you accept these rules.

This Code of Conduct supplements (it does not replace) **Discord's Community Guidelines** and the **JamSky [Terms of Service](https://jamskygames.com/terms)**.

---

## Eligibility

You must be **at least 16 years old** to participate in this server, consistent with our minimum age for the game. If you're under 16, please leave the server.

---

## The 5 core rules

### 1. Be respectful

Treat other members with basic respect. Disagreements about strategy, balance, or design are great. Personal attacks aren't.

**Not allowed:** harassment, threats, name-calling, slurs, sustained negativity directed at individuals.

### 2. No hate speech or discrimination

Content attacking people based on race, ethnicity, national origin, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, religion, disability, age, or other protected characteristics is not allowed.

### 3. Keep it safe and legal

**Not allowed:**
- Sexually explicit content (no NSFW, even in spoiler tags)
- Sexual content involving minors — this is a hard zero-tolerance ban + report to authorities
- Threats of violence or self-harm encouragement (we will direct people to crisis resources)
- Illegal content (piracy, malware, illegal drug sales, etc.)
- Doxxing or sharing other people's private information without consent
- Impersonating JamSky staff, moderators, or other community members

### 4. No spam, scams, or unwanted promotion

**Not allowed:**
- Repeated messages, all-caps blocks, mass mentions
- Phishing links, scams, or fake giveaway promotions
- Promoting other games, services, or Discord servers without prior approval
- Unsolicited DMs to other members for promotional purposes

### 5. Don't ruin the game for others

**Not allowed:**
- Sharing or promoting cheats, exploits, modified clients, or third-party software that affects gameplay
- Buying, selling, or trading accounts or virtual items
- Coordinating to abuse exploits or report-bomb other players
- Sharing leaked or unannounced game content (unless JamSky has publicly released it)

## Channel-specific rules

- **#announcements**: read-only; only JamSky staff post here
- **#general**: anything Incorgnito-related; basic chat

- **#bug-reports**: bug reports only; please include device and version

Channel pins may include additional rules specific to that channel.

## How to report

If you see a rule violation, you can:

1. **React** with the 🚩 emoji on the offending message — moderators will review
2. **Right-click → Report Message** in Discord
3. **Direct-message a moderator** (look for the "Mod" or "JamSky Staff" role)
4. **Email** [moderation@jamskygames.com](mailto:moderation@jamskygames.com) for sensitive issues

Reports are confidential to the moderation team. We do not share who reported what with the person being reported.

---

## What happens when rules are broken

Moderators apply consequences proportional to the violation:

| Violation level | Response |
|---|---|
| Minor (first-time, low-harm) | Verbal warning + remove message |
| Moderate or repeat | Warning + temporary mute (24-72 hours) |
| Serious or repeat-after-warning | Server kick |
| Severe (hate speech, threats, doxxing, exploit-sharing, age violation, sexual content involving minors) | Permanent ban |

Bans for severe violations may be paired with: revoking access to the in-game JamSky Discord link, in-game account review, and (where applicable) reports to law enforcement or relevant authorities.

**Appeals.** You may appeal a moderation action by emailing [moderation@jamskygames.com](mailto:moderation@jamskygames.com) within 30 days. Appeals are reviewed by a moderator who was not involved in the original action, within 14 days. You may submit additional context, evidence, or witness statements with your appeal. We will issue a written decision with a brief statement of reasoning. Where the appeal is denied and you believe our decision is unjustified, you may also seek out-of-court dispute resolution where required by applicable law.

**Statement of reasons.** When we take a moderation action against your account or content, we will provide a brief statement identifying which rule was violated, what content triggered the action, and what consequence applied — unless doing so would compromise an ongoing investigation, the safety of others, or be prohibited by law.
